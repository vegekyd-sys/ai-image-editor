# Vintage Film

Transform photos into vintage film aesthetic.

## Style Rules
- Warm amber/golden color temperature
- Visible but subtle film grain texture
- Soft highlight bloom (slightly overexposed highlights)
- Faded/lifted shadows (not pure black)
- Slightly desaturated greens and blues
- Enhanced warm skin tones
